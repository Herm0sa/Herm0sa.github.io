--Intro - GoodNotes good 在何处？

☞ Leave paper behind 真正实现无纸化

GoodNotes transforms your iPad into digital paper.
It's a single place for all your handwritten notes
and formerly paper-based information.

GoodNotes将你的iPad转换为数字纸张。
这里可以存放所有手写笔记和曾为纸质的信息。

☞ Always find what you wrote 你只管写，找就交给它

Thanks to powerful OCR technology,
everything in GoodNotes is searchable.
Find handwritten notes, PDF text,
document & folder titles, typed text, and outlines.

基于强大的OCR技术，
GoodNotes中的所有内容皆可搜索。
你可以查找手写笔记、PDF文本，
文档和文件夹标题、键入的文本和大纲。

☞ Enjoy the freedom of 手写随心所欲

Write and sketch with your finger or a stylus on
digital paper & imported documents. You can add
images, typed text and perfect shapes to the page
and reorder and resize them as you wish.
GoodNotes lets you escape the limits of real paper.

用手指或触控笔在数字纸张或导入的文件上写写画画。
你可以在页面上添加图像、键入的文本和完美的形状，
并根据需要重新排序或调整大小。
GoodNotes可以让您摆脱传统纸张的限制。

☞ Effortless document management 轻松管理文档

GoodNotes is a single place for all your documents.
Quickly import PDF, Word, and PowerPoint files to
annotate them or create a new blank notebook to
take notes. Organize and manage them with ease.
No document will ever be lost again.

所有文档都存放在GoodNotes这一个大文件包中。
你可以快速导入PDF、Word和PowerPoint文件
进行批注，或创建新的空白笔记本记笔记。
整理文件轻松自如。任何文件都不会再丢失。

☞ All your notes with you at all times 笔记随时与你同在

Write down ideas on the go and annotate photos.
You will always have your notes with you on
all of your devices thanks to iCloud sync.
Endless sheets of paper in your pocket.

你可以边走边写下想法，
给照片加上注解。
基于iCloud同步，
你的笔记会随时在你所有的苹果设备上更新。
这样就好像口袋里装了无数张纸一样。
